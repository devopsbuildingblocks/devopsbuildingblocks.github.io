About

Who's behind DevOps Building Blocks and why this site exists.

Jan 1, 0001

I’m Joshua Ward — a DevOps practitioner who spends a lot of time in the gap between “the docs say it works” and “it actually works in prod.”

This site is where I document that gap. Some posts are structured guides I wish had existed when I was figuring something out. Others are articles where I share more unfiltered thoughts about tools, tradeoffs, and the industry.

If something here saved you time or made you think differently about a problem, that’s the whole point.

Guides, articles, and hard-won lessons from the DevOps trenches.
